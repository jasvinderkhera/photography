A Little About Me

I am a professional photographer who grew up in Vancouver, Canada. As a child, I love to spending my days outdoors, playing and exploring. On rainy days, I would draw pictures of mountains and rivers. During my teen years, I discovered photography. I immediately fell in love with nature photography, and now, it is my passion.
